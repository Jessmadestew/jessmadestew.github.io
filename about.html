Bio

Growing up with an overactive imagination and a tendency to daydream, I’ve always expressed myself through creative modes, such as drawing or writing. I believe that, in numerous pieces, I’ve taken some sort of inspiration from my dreams and nightmares alike. This usually produces a unique, if not sometimes eerie, creation. Building upon the memories of passages I've read and scenes I've seen, I've created hundreds of stories that will most likely never possess a physical form.

I admire the works of Mark Ryden, Tim Burton, and Neil Gaiman.
